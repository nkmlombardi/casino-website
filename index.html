Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Why is Blockchain necessary for this game?

    Gambling is at the forefront of blockchain adoption, as it benefits greatly from removing the trust element from a casino. In many games (and even physical casinos), the odds of winning are tampered with. This creates mistrust between the player and the house, and ruins the gambling experience.

  • How will this game be immersive, why does that matter?

    We believe that people gamble primarily for the experience, not for the prospect of winning money. Millions of dollars of a casino's budget is poured into the experience that you get just from being there. The environment, lights and sounds are what fascinates casino visitors, and that is what we will emulate in our game.

  • How will your company tackle the many legal issues surrounding online gambling?

    One of the reasons we didn't launch last year, was because we were investigating the legal issues surround online gambling, especially with the use of Cryptocurrencies.

  • Why focus so heavily on the multiplayer and social interactions of the players?

    Humans are naturally social organisms. We place great value on the ability to play alongside your friends, or make new ones in your ventures throughout the game. Great efforts will be made to let players express themselves through their avatars, by buying cosmetic items using in-game chips.

  • What separates your product from competitors like FunFair, Etheroll, etc?

    The most obvious answer is that no one else is combining virtual reality / 3D with online gambling. By introducing this element, we create more than just a user interface where you bet money, and instead we create a world for you to explore and become part of. We really want the game to be driven by the social interactions between the players, and will focus heavily on the community.
